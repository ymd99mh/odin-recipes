Udon

Description

Udon is one of popular foods in Japan.

It's super simple and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • kombu and katsuobushi, or anchovy/sardine stock
  • Udon
  • Soy Sause
  • Green Onion
  • Beef
  • Tenkasu

Steps

  1. Take dashi from kombu and katsuobushi, or anchovy/sardine stock
  2. Season the dashi with soy sauce
  3. Head up Udon noodle for a minutes, and chill in extremely cold water
  4. Garnish the noodles with chopped green onion, beef, and tenkasu
