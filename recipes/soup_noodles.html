The Simplest Onigiri

Description

No time in the morning?

This is a healty opution for those who live in a busy life

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Seaweed

Steps

  1. Prepare the rice.
  2. Add a pinch of salt and mix everything to be even.
  3. Gentlly shape the rice with your hands to be a nicely rounded triangle.
  4. Lastly, Rap the rice with seaweed.
